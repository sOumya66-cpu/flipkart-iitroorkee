# Flipkart Gen Z Commerce Strategy

Product strategy case study developed for COMET '26 × Flipkart.

## Problem

Gen Z increasingly discovers products through creators, communities, and social platforms, causing Flipkart to lose influence before the purchase stage.

## Solution

Designed a three-part strategy:

* **Flipkart Circle**: Social collections and community-driven shopping
* **Trust Layer**: Price history, fit intelligence, and purchase-confidence signals
* **Creator Commerce**: Native creator storefronts and merchandise ecosystem

## Highlights

* Conducted market and competitor analysis
* Built Gen Z user personas using primary research
* Performed TAM–SAM–SOM market sizing
* Prioritized features using the RICE framework
* Designed a 12-month implementation roadmap
* Defined success metrics and risk mitigation plans
